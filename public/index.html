After School Programming Club

Introduction




Code After Hours is an organization that was founded by the idea of having Coding Clubs in Puerto Rico schools. Founded and being maintained by two high school students and a Psychologist/Web Designer. The main goal of Code After Hours is to provide mentors and help to high school students so they can implement coding clubs at their schools. Every week the organizers from all the different schools will have a meeting to discuss the topics they taught and the progress of each club. Through the school year, Code After Hours will be having monthly hackathons and anually hackatons in which the students will have a chance to win prices.

About



We've discovered that in Puerto Rico the public school system invests money on technology but the students learn how to be users instead of getting taught to be the creaters.


What are we doing about it? We created Code After Hours, a non-profitable organization that aims to help students learn how to code in every way possible.
